Privacy Policy

Search With ChatGPT does not collect, store, or share personal data.

What the extension does

The extension only adds a Chrome context-menu entry called “Buscar en ChatGPT”.

When the user selects text and clicks that option, the extension:

  1. reads the selected text;
  2. encodes it into a URL query;
  3. opens a new tab in ChatGPT.

Data practices

The extension does not:

  • collect browsing history,
  • store searches on developer servers,
  • use its own cookies,
  • send data to a developer backend or database,
  • sell or share user data with third parties.

Permissions used

  • contextMenus: to add the context menu item.
  • tabs: to open a new tab with ChatGPT.
